The story begins with two artists, one a painter and one a musician, who are in a codependent relationship. The painter is obsessed with the musician and the musician is constantly trying to escape the painter's grasp. The story follows the ups and downs of their tumultuous relationship as each tries to control the other. In the end, the musician finally breaks free from the painter and leaves him for good. The painter is left heartbroken and devastated, but eventually finds new love and happiness with someone else.
