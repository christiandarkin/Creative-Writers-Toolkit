The story begins with two young, struggling artists who meet and fall in love. They are both codependent on each other and their art, and they soon realize that they can't live without each other. They move in together and their relationship is tumultuous but passionate. They both continue to struggle with their art, but they find solace in each other. Eventually, they both start to find success with their art. They get married and have a child. Their life is happy and content, but they both still feel chained to their art. They can't imagine life without each other or their art. One day, tragedy strikes and one of them dies. The other is left with their art, but it feels empty without the person they loved. They eventually find solace in their art again, but it will never be the same without the person they loved.
